Why Recycle?

In the age of persistent discovery, it can be easy to forget the electronics we keep around when we replace our old tech with the "next big thing". The truth is, keeping around old electrical and electronic equipment like batteries can be very harmful. Most electronics contain hazardous materials that become harmful as the chemicals inside decay. Keeping them around for too long without proper disposal is dangerous; some chemical compounds that are used in these electronics are initially benign in nature, but grow to become hazardous over time.
